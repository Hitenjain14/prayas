About Us

Founded in September 2021, PRAYAS: Punjab Regional Association of Youth for Advancement of Sciences is a community of enthusiastic students, researchers and academicians working for science popularization among students and general public. PRAYAS will serve as a platform for the manifestation of scientific aptitude in students and public while indulging in healthy competitions and other scientific activities. The association brings together students, teachers, college and university lecturers, professors, scientists and researchers from industry for organizing science promotion activities.

Scope of The Event

A novel initiative of PRAYAS, to give a real time experience to the students regarding internships, is to be started from Amritsar district in the year 2020-21. This project will help in bridging a gap between UG/PG/PhD students and school students.

  • A group of two-three students will be assigned to particular guide to work on a specific problem in any of the sciences i.e. physical, biological or Chemical Sciences. Guide will be an Undergraduate or a Postgraduate student from any institution.
  • Lectures in Blended mode by eminent personalities.
  • Visit to Faculty of Sciences at Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar
